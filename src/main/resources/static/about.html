About HAPPIDO

Welcome to HAPPIDO, your premier destination for reliable, safe, and convenient cab bookings. Born from a vision to revolutionize urban transportation, HAPPIDO is committed to providing an unparalleled travel experience for every passenger. We believe that getting around should be effortless, enjoyable, and accessible to everyone.

Our Story

HAPPIDO was founded in 2023 by a team of passionate innovators who saw an opportunity to improve the daily commute for millions. Frustrated by unreliable services and opaque pricing, they envisioned a platform that prioritized user experience, safety, and transparency. From humble beginnings with a handful of drivers and a simple app, HAPPIDO has grown into a trusted name, serving communities across various cities. Our journey has been driven by continuous learning, technological advancement, and an unwavering focus on our customers.

Our Mission

Our mission at HAPPIDO is to connect passengers with professional drivers through a seamless, intuitive platform, ensuring every journey is comfortable, timely, and secure. We strive to enhance daily commutes and travel experiences by leveraging cutting-cutting-edge technology and a deep understanding of our users' needs.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred cab booking service globally, recognized for our commitment to safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where transportation is not just a means to an end, but a delightful part of your day.
