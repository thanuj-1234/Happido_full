Frequently Asked Questions

For Passengers:

Q: How do I book a ride?

A: You can book a ride directly through our HAPPIDO mobile app, available on iOS and Android. Simply enter your pick-up and drop-off locations, choose your preferred cab type, and confirm your booking.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and cash payments (where available). You can manage your payment options within the app.

Q: How can I track my driver?

A: Once your ride is confirmed, you can track your driver's location in real-time on the map within the HAPPIDO app. You'll also receive updates on their estimated arrival time.

Q: Can I schedule a ride in advance?

A: Yes, HAPPIDO allows you to schedule rides for a later time or date. This feature is perfect for airport transfers or important appointments. Just select the 'Schedule Ride' option in the app.

Q: How do I update my profile information or payment details?

A: You can easily update your personal and payment information by navigating to the 'Profile' or 'Settings' section within the HAPPIDO mobile application. Ensure all details are current for a smooth experience.

For Drivers:

Q: How can I become a HAPPIDO driver?

A: To become a HAPPIDO driver, you need to meet certain eligibility criteria including age, driving experience, and vehicle requirements. Visit our 'Drive with Us' section on the website or app to start your application process.

Q: What are the vehicle requirements for drivers?

A: Vehicle requirements vary by region but generally include a modern, well-maintained vehicle that meets safety standards. Specific details can be found in the driver application portal or by contacting driver support.

Q: How and when do drivers get paid?

A: HAPPIDO offers flexible payment options, typically processed weekly or bi-weekly via direct deposit. Drivers can track their earnings and payment history directly through the driver app.

Q: Is there support for drivers?

A: Absolutely! HAPPIDO provides 24/7 driver support through the app, phone, and email. We also have dedicated driver hubs in major cities for in-person assistance and community building.
